Dinner is considered by several, as the most important meal in the daily cycle of meals. While most households have tended to share the main daily meal during daylight hours, some societies have pushed dinner to the evenings, making it the most important meal.


Beyond serving as a family gathering, a dinner can be elaborate and often the major meals during the year (Christmas, Thanksgiving) are identified as dinner.
